Inside every capsule

Three actives. Three distinct roles.

Rather than relying on broad, non-specific liver herbs, DHM 247 is designed around distinct but complementary biological roles.

— 01 / DHM

Dihydromyricetin

Alcohol-processing & oxidative balance

A flavonoid isolated from Nekemias grossedentata (Vine Tea), traditionally used in East Asian medicine. DHM supports the activity and balance of enzymes involved in the metabolism of ethanol and acetaldehyde.

Mechanism Modulates Nrf2 antioxidant response and supports endogenous defense enzymes including superoxide dismutase and glutathione peroxidase.
— 02 / CHOLINE

Choline

Hepatic lipid transport & cellular integrity

An essential nutrient required for normal liver function and lipid metabolism. Serves as a precursor to phosphatidylcholine — a major structural component of hepatocyte membranes.

Mechanism Supports the export of triglycerides from the liver via VLDL formation and contributes methyl groups for normal detoxification pathways.
— 03 / PIPERINE

Piperine

Bioavailability & metabolic support

A naturally occurring alkaloid from Piper nigrum, widely studied for enhancing the bioavailability of polyphenols and other plant-derived compounds.

Mechanism Influences intestinal absorption and metabolic turnover, supporting consistent systemic exposure to active compounds.
